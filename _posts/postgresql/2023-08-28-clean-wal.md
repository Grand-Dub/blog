**Si pas d'archivage des wal**

*nettoyage:*
```sql
do $$ begin
  for counter in 1..(select count(*)-1 from pg_ls_dir('pg_wal'))
  loop
    perform pg_walfile_name(pg_switch_wal());
    checkpoint;
  end loop;
end; $$;
```
